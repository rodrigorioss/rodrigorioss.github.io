History

The Beatles were an iconic British rock band formed in Liverpool in 1960.

Members

The original members of The Beatles were John Lennon, Paul McCartney, George Harrison, and Ringo Starr.

Music

The band produced numerous hit songs and albums, including "Sgt. Pepper's Lonely Hearts Club Band," "Abbey Road," and "Let It Be."

Impact

The Beatles had a profound influence on the music industry and popular culture, often referred to as one of the greatest bands of all time.

Fun Facts

Their appearance on "The Ed Sullivan Show" in 1964 marked the start of Beatlemania in the United States.
